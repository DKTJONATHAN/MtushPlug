About Mtush Plug KE

Our story and mission to provide affordable fashion

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Mtush Plug KE began as a small initiative to provide high-quality second-hand clothing to Nairobi residents at affordable prices. What started as a side hustle quickly grew into a full-fledged business as we saw the demand for sustainable fashion options in Kenya.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality clothing without breaking the bank. Our mission is to:

  • Provide affordable pre-loved fashion to our customers
  • Promote sustainable shopping habits
  • Offer carefully curated items in excellent condition
  • Create a seamless shopping experience

Why Choose Us

Quality Assurance

Every item is carefully inspected to ensure it meets our high standards before being listed.

Affordable Prices

We offer prices that are 50-80% lower than retail for similar quality items.

Reliable Delivery

Fast and secure delivery options across Nairobi with flexible pickup options.
