Our Story

Founded in 2023, Mtush Plug KE began as a small initiative to provide high-quality second-hand clothing to Nairobi residents at affordable prices. What started as a side hustle quickly grew into a full-fledged business as we saw the demand for sustainable fashion options in Kenya.

We're passionate about making fashion accessible while reducing textile waste in our community.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality clothing without breaking the bank

Sustainable Fashion
